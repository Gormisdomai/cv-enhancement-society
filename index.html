Oxford CV Enhancement Society

What We Do

We know you're busy. So we created the perfect society for ambitious, talented, and driven individuals at the University of Oxford who want to take a leadership role in student life - but without taking on another burdensome commitment.

With the CV enhancement society, you don’t need to come to any taster sessions, tryouts, trainings, talks, rehearsals, socials, drinks, dinners, meetings, or networking events. To maximise our members’ leadership potential, we have a rotating presidency: every day a new member becomes the President of the Society.

FAQ

Is this real?

Unfortunately, yes.

What qualifications do I need to become a member?

None

How was the CV enhancement society founded?

It was founded at University College as Societas Oxoniensis Curriculum Vitae Amplificationem. The exact date of foundation is disputed, but most sources agree it must have been closer to 1834 than to 1812.

Can I put this on my CV?

Yes, in fact you are contractually obligated to do so. Violation will result in immediate impeachment.

How can I make my society an oxford cv enhancement society-certified student organisation?

Contact your society's secretary and encourage them to apply through our sister organisation, the Oxford Society Enhancement Society.

Am I allowed to hold office in another society as well as the oxford cv enhancement society?

Yes, this is encouraged. In fact, the president of the oxford cv enhancement society is also president of the Oxford Society Enhancement Society and its governing body, Oxford Enhancement Societies, Ltd.
